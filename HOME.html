ABOUT US

It becomes a very tideous jobs for the administration to teach and conduct examination during some unprecedented situations , the one we experienced during the lockdowns. These system provides an efficient way to ensure smooth functioning of the educational sector by incorporating into it the use of online milieu.

By this system any institute can register themselves and add their courses in the website wherein teachers and students can interact with each other and at the end of the compeltion of the course , institute can conduct their online examination . This could be a great boon to the education system as it saves lot of time and resources .

This website allows the institute admin to create courses and assign students and teachers to a course. Also allows the examiner to create and evaluate an exam in a proctored manner. The students can attempt the exam and view the grade sheet for a particular course, semester or cumulatively.
